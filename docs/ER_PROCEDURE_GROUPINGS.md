# Common ER Procedure Groupings

This document lists common Emergency Room procedure groupings for testing and reference.

## 1. Trauma/Laceration Care
- Laceration Repair
- Wound Debridement
- Suturing
- Staple Removal
- Wound Irrigation

## 2. Fracture/Orthopedic
- Closed Reduction
- Splinting
- Casting
- Joint Dislocation Reduction
- X-ray Interpretation

## 3. Airway Management
- Intubation / Endotracheal Intubation
- Bag-Valve Mask (BVM)
- Rapid Sequence Intubation (RSI)
- Cricothyrotomy
- Oxygen Administration

## 4. Cardiac Emergencies
- CPR / ACLS
- Defibrillation / Cardioversion
- 12-Lead ECG
- Central Line Placement
- Pericardiocentesis

## 5. IV Access & Fluids
- Peripheral IV Insertion
- Central Venous Catheter (CVC)
- Intraosseous (IO) Access
- Blood Transfusion
- Fluid Resuscitation

## 6. Respiratory
- Chest X-ray
- Nebulizer Treatment
- Chest Tube / Thoracostomy
- Thoracentesis
- BiPAP/CPAP

## 7. GI/Abdominal
- NG Tube Insertion
- Gastric Lavage
- Paracentesis
- Foley Catheter Insertion
- Rectal Exam

## 8. Neuro Emergencies
- Lumbar Puncture (LP)
- CT Head
- Stroke Assessment (tPA administration)
- Seizure Management

## 9. Toxicology/Overdose
- Activated Charcoal
- Naloxone (Narcan) Administration
- Gastric Lavage
- Toxicology Screen

## 10. Minor Procedures
- Foreign Body Removal
- Abscess Incision & Drainage (I&D)
- Burn Care/Dressing
- Eye Irrigation
- Ear Lavage
